Music Group Recommendations

The purpose of this study is to analyze the satisfaction related to listening to different songs in different contexts, alone or in a group. Nava Tintarev (Full Professor at Maastricht University, DACS) and Francesco Barile (Assistant Professor at Maastricht University, DACS) are conducting this evaluation.
Your participation in this research study is voluntary. You may choose not to participate. If you decide to participate in this research survey, you may withdraw at any time. If you do not complete the survey, we will treat it as a withdrawal and your answers thus far will not be used.
The procedure involves filling out two online surveys in two different sessions, of about 30 minutes each. The second session will start in 10 days. What to expect?
  • You will need a Google account to participate in this study. You will be asked to register on our web application using your Google account.
  • {% if session['invited'] %} You will perform the experiment together with the person who invited you (each one will carry out the experiment individually). This should be somebody with whom you have a close relationship (e.g., your partner or a close friend). {% else %} You also will be asked to indicate the email address and the nickname of a second person who can perform the experiment with you (each one will carry out the experiment individually). This should be somebody with whom you have a close relationship (e.g., your partner or a close friend). {% endif %}
  • You will be asked to provide personality evaluations for both you and your friend. Such evaluations will be confidential and stored anonymously. Furthermore, you will be asked to provide several evaluations related to songs listening in different contexts.
  • Please note that these songs evaluations will be shared with your friend and (anonymously) with a second randomly selected participant. The random participant will have no personal information to associate such evaluations with you.
  • It is necessary for our purposes that both of you are available to perform the required sessions during the next weeks.
  • You will receive an email notifying you of the start of session two on the email address you indicate in the survey.
All data is stored in a password protected electronic format. We will not store any information that will personally identify you, including IP addresses. {% if session['invited'] %} Your email address is the only information we store in a not anonymized way. We will only use your email address for notification purposes. {% else %} Your email address is the only information we store in a not anonymized way, and shared only with your friend to let them know who is inviting them. Then, we will only use your email address for notification purposes. {% endif %} After the end of the second session, you will have 30 days to contact us to delete your data; then, we will delete all the email addresses and nicknames, and all the provided data will be stored anonymously. The results of this study will be used for the creation of scholarly publication. Furthermore, a public dataset will be generated and made available to the scientific community; in this case, only anonymized data will be included in such dataset.
