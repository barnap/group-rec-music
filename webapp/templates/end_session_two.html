Music Group Recommendations

Thanks for your participation in our experiment.
The aim of this research is to assess whether the satisfaction of listening to a particular song is influenced by the presence of a second person and the knowledge of his/her ratings. We intend to determine whether there are certain types of people who are more likely to be influenced and differently appreciate a song in such a context, in relation to their personality and the type of relationship they have with their travelling companion.
In case you need more information about our experiment, please feel free to contact us.
